Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a standard porta potty rental cost?

Average porta potty rental prices range from $85–$130/week. Final cost depends on location, duration, and service frequency, especially in high-demand states like Texas or Florida.

Can I get a porta potty delivered today?

Yes. Same day porta potty rental is available in most metropolitan areas in California, Florida, Texas, Georgia, and Arizona, subject to availability.

Do you offer ADA units nationwide?

Yes. ADA compliant portable toilets are available in all 50 states, with same-week delivery in most regions.

How often are construction site units serviced?

Standard service is once per week for crews of 10+. High-traffic sites may require twice-weekly cleanings, customized per project nationwide.

What’s the minimum rental period?

One day for standard and residential units. Some luxury or trailer rentals require 2–3 days, but not for basic portable toilet rental.

A Shawnee village was built in the area by war chief Blue Jacket after the 1795 Treaty of Greenville. The area later became the site of the Battle of Monguagon between Americans and a British-Indian coalition during the War of 1812. The battle is commemorated with a Michigan State Historical Site marker in present-day Elizabeth Park, which was the first county park in Michigan when it was established in 1919.
